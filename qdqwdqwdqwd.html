attractions in New York

The Metropolitan Museum of Art, MET

The Met is the largest and one of the most prestigious art museums in the United States, located on Fifth Avenue in New York City.

Times Square

Known for its digital billboards, theaters, and energy, Times Square is one of NYC’s most iconic locations.

Statue of Liberty

A gift from France, the Statue of Liberty stands as a symbol of freedom in New York Harbor.

Rockefeller Center

A hub for business, media, and holiday festivities, including the famous Christmas Tree Lighting.
